🎯 ATS Optimization Tips
🔑 Use keywords from the job description naturally in your summary and skills.
📊 Quantify results: "Increased performance by 40%" beats "Improved performance".
📁 Use standard headings: "Experience", "Education", "Skills" are ATS-friendly.
📏 Keep it to 1 page if you have less than 3 years of experience.
🔗 Always include your GitHub and LinkedIn — tech recruiters always check.
✅ Use action verbs: Built, Developed, Led, Designed, Optimized, Deployed.
✅ Tailor your resume for each job application for best results.
